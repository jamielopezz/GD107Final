Hello, I'm Jamie Lopez — a Graphic Design student with a passion for Brand Identity and UX/UI Design.


I thrive on combining creativity with strategy to craft designs that are not only visually engaging but also intuitive and user-focused. My work spans a variety of mediums, from digital interfaces and web design to print and packaging — allowing me to adapt my approach based on the needs of each project and each client.

As a flexible and versatile designer, I enjoy stepping into new challenges and exploring different creative directions. I'm also a curious learner, constantly experimenting with new tools and staying up-to-date with industry trends to sharpen my skills and evolve as a designer.

I have experience digitally with Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Procreate, etc. I have also developed skills in Web Design using HTML and CSS. I also am experienced with physical art, like drawing, crocheting, and sewing.
